I am Berk Mervan Erenci, developer.

As a blockchain developer, my journey began with Near Protocol, where I quickly distinguished myself as one of the first 200 Near Protocol Certified Developers. While I initially honed my skills in this innovative ecosystem, my curiosity for exploration led me to explore vast potential of Ethereum. Embracing this transition, I immersed myself in the Ethereum ecosystem, gaining extensive knowledge in developing smart contracts, adhering to ERC standards, and leveraging the power of web3 tools to create decentralized applications.

At Loodic Games, I led the development of platform, creating a user-friendly frontend and backend, implementing secure smart contracts using Next.js, Ethers.js, Solidity and Firebase. I collaborated closely with the team, inspired by the project's potential to transform the gaming industry.

Beyond these, I have been an active participant in fostering various blockchain communities. I played a vital role in nurturing the Near University, Patika, and Kodluyoruz communities, where I've shared my experiences with aspiring developers, helping them navigate the complexities of blockchain development and inspiring the next generation of blockchain enthusiasts.

I take pride in my ability to adapt to the dynamic nature of the blockchain space, continuously learning and evolving to keep pace with the ever-changing technological landscape.
